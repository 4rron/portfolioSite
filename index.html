Who Am I?

A software engineer with a passion for stunning visual experiences, I go above and beyond just making an application work.
To me, nothing is worse than something that works well but looks awful or feels unintuitive to the user. That’s why I’m constantly looking into new design trends and trying to find ways to combine them with new ideas on how to make data friendly.
